About

Senior Software Engineering Consultant on Shutterstock Contributor team.

Maker at FiftyThree. Software Engineer at Zynga Seattle. Co-Founder of The Thingdom. Developer at Bing, working on Search User Experience. Former developer at Microsoft Live Labs, working on Zoom.it and related Seadragon projects.

Received my ETH BSc in Computer Science in May, 2010. Former intern at Microsoft Live Labs and Zoomorama.
